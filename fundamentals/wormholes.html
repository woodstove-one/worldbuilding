Wormholes

Formation

A wormhole is the result of a black hole that warped space far enough to fuse with at least another universe.

Types

The wormhole classification is simple. The first letter represents the main type of wormhole. The second letter represents the number of connections: A is for one connection and B is for more than one connection.

Type-C

Type Connecting: They connect at least two universes together.

Type-R

Type Returning: They connect at least two points in the same universe.

Type-O

Type Open: They are open-ended and allow access to higher dimensions.
