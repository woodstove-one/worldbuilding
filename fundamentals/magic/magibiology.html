Magibiology

Organelles

Dimensioholder

Description

Dimensioholders capture and transport magic to magivaults.

Physiology

Dimensioholders are organelles with magic sensors that use localized spacetime distortions. They travel randomly through the host's body. When they encounter a magic particle, it is trapped by a special structure. This causes the dimensioholder to have a different charge. It is attracted towards the corresponding magivaults.

Organs

Magivault

Description

The magivault stores magical energy for later use.

Physiology

The exomembrane of the magivault has channels with a charge opposite of the energy it stores. This attracts dimensioholders. Once the dimensioholders reach the channels, they are sucked in by a stronger attractor, the endoattractor. The endoattractor holds the magic securely until magic is needed.
